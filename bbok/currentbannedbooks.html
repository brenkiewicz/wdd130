Where to Find These Books Check out your local public libraries for these titles! Your library may also have a digital database for checking out eBooks, which is another great resource for finding this titles, such as: Libby Hoopla Cloudlibrary
If you want, you can purchase these books through many sellers, including secondhand. And you can look for past banned books that are now public domain on websites like Librivox and FeedBooks!
